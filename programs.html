Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our programs? Here are answers to the most common inquiries.

Do I need any prior computer experience for the ICT program?

+

No! Our ICT program is designed for complete beginners. We start with the absolute basics and gradually build your skills to an intermediate level. All you need is the willingness to learn.

What is included in the KES 1,500 fee?

+

The fee covers all training materials, access to our computer lab and nail tech workspace, internet access (for ICT students), practical sessions, and your certification upon completion. There are no hidden charges.

Can I take both programs at the same time?

+

Yes, you can enroll in both programs. However, we recommend focusing on one at a time for the best learning experience. You can enroll in the second program after completing the first.

What certification do I receive upon completion?

+

You receive a Kirimari Empowerment Centre completion certificate, validated through our partnership with Kangaru VTC. ICT students also benefit from Ajira Kenya certifications and platform access.

How do I enroll in a program?

+

Enrollment is simple! Contact us via WhatsApp at 0710751420, visit our centre next to Wells Fargo near Urban Primary School, or visit our enrollment page. You'll need your birth certificate to register.
