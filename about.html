Our Identity

Who We Are

Kirimari Empowerment Centre is a community-based vocational and digital training centre located in the heart of Kirimari Ward, Manyatta Constituency, Embu County, Kenya.

We serve as a beacon of hope and opportunity for youth and community members seeking practical skills that lead to real employment and self-reliance. Our unique approach combines traditional vocational training with modern digital skills, creating a comprehensive pathway to economic empowerment.

A Learning Hub

Providing digital literacy and vocational training to our community

An Economic Enabler

Creating pathways to job creation and self-employment

A Digital Access Point

Providing internet access, online services, and Ajira opportunities

Purpose-Driven

Our Vision & Mission

Everything we do is guided by our commitment to empowering youth and transforming lives through practical skills training.

Our Vision

"Empowering youth through technology and vocational skills for a better future."

We envision a community where every young person has access to the skills and opportunities needed to build a prosperous, self-reliant future.

Our Mission

"To provide accessible training in ICT and nail technology, equipping individuals with practical skills for employment and self-reliance."

We deliver hands-on, affordable training programs that transform beginners into skilled practitioners ready to earn and thrive.

Core Purpose

Why We Exist

The centre exists to address youth unemployment, provide practical income-generating skills, bridge the digital skills gap, and promote entrepreneurship and self-reliance in our community.

Our Journey

Growth Timeline

From our founding to the present day, every milestone represents lives transformed and communities empowered.

Foundation

Centre Established

Kirimari Empowerment Centre was founded in Kirimari Ward with the vision of empowering youth through practical skills.

Early Growth

First ICT Cohorts

Launched our ICT & Digital Skills program, completing Cohorts 1-3 with growing community participation and impact.

Expansion

Nail Technology Launch

Introduced the Nail Technology Program, expanding our offering to include vocational skills alongside digital training.

Partnerships

Ajira Kenya & Kangaru VTC

Formed key partnerships with Ajira Kenya Program and Kangaru VTC, enhancing our certification and providing online employment pathways.

Present Day

150+ Students & Growing

Having completed 6 ICT cohorts and 2 Nail Tech cohorts, we continue to grow our impact with 150+ students trained and empowered.

Community Impact

Our Role in The Community

Kirimari Empowerment Centre serves multiple vital roles in our community, acting as a catalyst for positive change and development.

A Learning Hub

We serve as the community's primary center for digital literacy and vocational training, providing accessible education to all.

An Economic Enabler

By creating skilled graduates, we drive job creation and self-employment, contributing directly to local economic growth.

A Digital Access Point

We provide internet access, online services, and Ajira opportunities, bridging the digital divide in our community.
