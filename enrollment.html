Important Information

Before You Enroll

Class Schedule

Classes run Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Regular attendance is essential for successful completion.

Payment

The KES 1,500 fee is a one-time payment covering the entire program duration including materials and certification.

Certification

Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate validated through our partnership with Kangaru VTC.

No Experience Needed

Our programs are designed for complete beginners. We start from the basics and guide you to proficiency.
