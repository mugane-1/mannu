Life at Kirimari Centre

From classroom sessions to graduation celebrations, witness the journey of transformation at our empowerment centre.

ICT Training Session

Students engaged in hands-on computer training during our Cohort 5 ICT program. Learning Microsoft Office and digital literacy fundamentals.

Nail Art Workshop

Creative nail art designs being practiced during our vocational training sessions. Students learn professional techniques from expert trainers.

Graduation Ceremony

Our graduates celebrating their achievements after completing the ICT & Digital Skills program. A proud moment for students and trainers alike.

Community Engagement

Building connections within Kirimari Ward. Our centre serves as a hub for youth empowerment and community development activities.

Digital Literacy Class

Students learning essential computer skills including typing, internet navigation, and basic software applications for the modern workplace.

Manicure Practice Session

Hands-on practice in professional manicure techniques including nail shaping, cuticle care, and polish application.

Orientation Day

New students being welcomed and oriented during the start of a fresh cohort. Introduction to centre facilities and program expectations.

Student Testimonials

Graduates sharing their transformation stories and how the skills gained at Kirimari Centre changed their lives and career prospects.

Computer Lab Session

Our fully-equipped computer lab where students practice typing, spreadsheet management, and online job applications.

Nail Art Designs

Creative nail art patterns and decorations created by our talented students. Showcasing the artistic skills developed during training.

Certification Day

Students receiving their certificates of completion. A milestone moment marking their journey from beginners to skilled practitioners.

Achievement Celebration

Celebrating milestones and achievements together. Building a supportive community of learners and future entrepreneurs.

Online Jobs Training

Learning to navigate online job platforms through the Ajira Kenya Program. Preparing students for digital freelancing opportunities.

Pedicure Services Training

Professional pedicure techniques including foot care, massage, and nail treatment. Students practice on real clients under supervision.

Group Training Sessions

Interactive group learning sessions where students collaborate, share ideas, and learn from each other's experiences.

Empowering the next generation with practical skills that lead to employment, entrepreneurship, and self-reliance in the digital age.

Excel Training

Mastering Microsoft Excel for data management, calculations, and professional reporting. A key skill for office employment.
