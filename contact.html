Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the fastest way to contact you?

+

WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us. Simply send a message to 0725642374 and we'll respond as soon as possible, typically within a few hours during working days.

Can I visit the centre before enrolling?

+

Absolutely! We welcome prospective students to visit our centre during working hours (Monday-Friday, 8AM-4PM). You can see our facilities, meet our trainers, and get all your questions answered in person.

Where exactly are you located?

+

We are located in Kirimari Ward, Manyatta Constituency, Embu County, Kenya. Our centre is next to Wells Fargo and near Urban Primary School. You can find us on Google Maps for exact directions.

Do you offer evening or weekend classes?

+

Currently, our classes run during regular working hours (Monday-Friday, 8AM-4PM). However, we're always looking for ways to serve our community better. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
